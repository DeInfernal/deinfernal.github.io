About me

Hello everyone!

My career as programmer/engineer started when I turned 10, and I got my first computer. From that moment I found many attractive technologies that make everyones life easier by usage of computational devices. Initially I was interested in security algorythms, but as time progressed, I found myself fascinated with neural networks and artificial intelligence as well.

Computers opened a whole new world for me. World of pure logic and intellect. And, prehaps, a little bit of magic and wizardy - because with computers and robotics, you can cure diseases that humanity couldn't tackle for so long, like blindness or even paralysis.

So here I am. Armed with knowledge and modern technologies, I can change the world. Under my command, computers will be able to calculate waypoints to launch rockets to space, find a cure for every single illness, and make humanity free from burden of survival. This is my goal.
